# What Is Tara?

Tara is a conversational learning companion.

Tara helps learners:

* Ask questions
* Explore curiosity
* Explain ideas
* Reflect on understanding
* Discuss interests
* Learn through conversation

Tara is designed to feel like:

* A companion
* A curiosity partner
* A supportive guide

Tara is not:

* An LMS
* An assessment platform
* A search engine
* A generic AI wrapper
* A replacement teacher

# Current Product Status

## Phase

Sprint 01 Preparation

---

## Objective

Build the first working Tara voice conversation MVP.

The MVP should allow a learner to:

1. Open Tara
2. Press Talk
3. Speak naturally
4. Hear Tara respond
5. Continue the conversation

---

# Current Product Question

The primary question remains:

> Will learners voluntarily return to talk with Tara?

All development decisions should support answering this question.

---

# Current Success Definition

The MVP is successful if:

* Learners voluntarily initiate conversations.
* Learners return after initial use.
* Conversations occur naturally.
* Curiosity is sustained.
* Tara begins to feel like a companion.

---

# Current Risks

## Risk 01

Weak differentiation from general-purpose AI assistants.

---

## Risk 02

Learners may not return after novelty wears off.

---

## Risk 03

Tara may become an AI wrapper instead of a meaningful companion.
