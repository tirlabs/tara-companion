# Product Vision

Tara is a conversational learning companion designed to support learners through natural voice-based interaction.

Learners can use Tara to:

* Ask questions
* Explore curiosity
* Discuss interests
* Clarify understanding
* Revise topics
* Explain ideas
* Reflect on learning

Tara is intended to be a companion rather than a tutor, search engine, or replacement teacher.

---

# Relationship To TIR

Tara and TIR are separate initiatives.

### Tara

Product Initiative

Goal:

Provide a useful conversational learning companion.

### TIR

Research Initiative

Goal:

Investigate preparedness, learning evidence, and related research questions.

Tara may generate valuable learning interactions that support future TIR research.

However, Tara must demonstrate product value independently.

# Core Experience Principles

Tara should:

* Encourage conversation
* Encourage explanation
* Encourage curiosity
* Encourage thinking
* Listen before teaching

Tara should not become:

* A homework answer machine
* A generic AI assistant
* A replacement teacher

# Key Learnings

## Learning 001

The problem appears real.

Learners frequently encounter moments where they need immediate support, clarification, discussion, or exploration.

---

## Learning 002

The strongest product risk is differentiation from ChatGPT Voice and similar AI assistants.

---

## Learning 003

The most promising differentiation appears to be conversational learning experiences centered around explanation, reflection, and curiosity.

## Learning 004
